Anemia is a medical condition in which the body lacks enough healthy red blood cells or hemoglobin to carry adequate oxygen to the tissues. This can result in a variety of symptoms, such as fatigue, weakness, pale skin, and shortness of breath. Anemia can be caused by many factors, including nutritional deficiencies, chronic diseases, or genetic conditions. The symptoms include fatigue, dizziness,shortness of breath..
